Pasta with Cheese Based Sauces

Cacio e Pepe

Ingredients

  1. 16 oz of Gluten Free Noodles (your choice)
  2. 1 1/2 cups Pecorino Romano cheese (Locatelli)
  3. 1 1/2 teaspoons corsely ground black pepper
  4. Starchy water used to boil the noodles (adjust as needed)

Instructions

Cacio e pepe is one of the most basic pasta dishes to make but the difference between terrible and tasty Cacio e Pepe comes down to technique. Italian chef Marco Rufini has been kind enough to share the secret to making world class Cacio e Pepe. This youtube video is in Italian but the important thing to focus on is the technique for making the cheese sauce.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  1. 16 oz of Gluten Free Noodles (your choice)
  2. 1 1/2 cups Pecorino Romano cheese (Locatelli)
  3. 3/4 Cup Pancetta or Guanciale (Bacon should only be used as a last resort)
  4. 2 Eggs
    • 1 whole egg
    • 1 egg yolk
  5. Starchy water used to boil the noodles (adjust as needed)
  6. White Onion - diced (Optional. Italian's do not include this)

Instructions

Pasta Carbonara is my personal favorite pasta Dish. Simply put, this dish is amazing! Once again we owe a big thank you to Italian chef Marco Rufini. In this video, he shows us the secret to making world class Carbonara. This youtube video is in Italian but the important thing to focus on is the technique for making the cheese sauce.
